# Security Policy

The integrity of the CureCoin protocol is our primary focus. We appreciate the community’s role in auditing the code and maintaining a secure environment.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you identify a potential bug or vulnerability in the logic of the protocol, please **do not** open a public issue. 

Please report technical findings privately to **info@curecoin.net**.

### Reporting Guidelines

To help us prioritize protocol maintenance, please include:
* **Technical Summary:** A concise description of the logic flaw.
* **Reproduction:** Clear steps to trigger the behavior.
* **Impact:** The effect on network stability or protocol execution.

### Our Approach

Reports are reviewed on a best-effort basis as development cycles permit. We prioritize issues that pose a direct risk to the integrity of the CureCoin network. We ask that you maintain confidentiality until a resolution is implemented to ensure the continued stability of the protocol for all participants.

Thank you for contributing to the security of the CureCoin network.
